Here is a list of modern agricultural machines and a brief explanation of each:

  1. Tractors

    2. Tractors are vehicles with large wheels used for plowing fields, tilling soil, and performing other farm-related tasks.  

  2. Harvesters

    3. Harvesters are machines used for harvesting crops such as wheat, rice, and corn. They can cut, thresh, and clean the crops all in one pass. 

  3. Plows

    4.   Plows are used for breaking up and turning over soil in preparation for planting.

  4. Cultivators

    5.   Cultivators are machines that are used to loosen and aerate soil and remove weeds.

  5. Sprayers

    6.   Sprayers are machines used to apply pesticides and fertilizers to crops.

  6. Irrigation

    7.   Irrigation systems are used to supply water to crops in order to improve yields and support growth.

  7. Planters

    8. Planters are machines used to plant seeds in the fields.

  8. Bale Wrappers

    9.   Bale wrappers are machines used to wrap large round bales of hay, straw, or silage.

  9. Loaders

    10. Loaders are machines used to load and unload materials such as hay bales, grain, and other supplies.  

  10. Drones

    11.   Drones equipped with cameras, sensors, and other technologies are becoming increasingly popular in agriculture for tasks such as crop monitoring and mapping.
